Who We Are

At Lightly, we're driven by a mission to revolutionize energy management through DePIN (Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Networks), making it smarter, more efficient, and accessible for everyone. We believe that every homeowner and business should have full control over their energy usage, reduce waste, and enjoy the benefits of sustainable power management. With our innovative intelligent distribution box, we empower users to optimize energy consumption, lower costs, and contribute to a greener, decentralized future.
